Grading Policy

Assignment Percent of Final Grade Description
Quizzes 10% 3 quizzes (5% each; 1 lowest score dropped)
Midterm Exam 20% T/F, Multiple Choices, Short Answers, Free Responses
Labs 40% 4 lab assignments (10% each; Final Project Proposal as Lab #4)
Final Project 20% Team project; each group to build a web application and present
Attendance & Discussion 10% In-class problem solving & discussion; random attendance check

Grading Scale

Grade Quantitative Grade

B+, B, B- Good 80-89%
C+, C Satisfactory 70-79%

Course Policies

  • Class engagement and attendance are important parts of the final grade; excused absences must be arranged at least 24 hours in advance of the class start time, and the evidence of the reason must be provided.
  • No late work will be accepted without permission in advance from the instructor before the due date.
  • No make-up test will be provided without permission in advance from the instructor.
  • All work submitted under your name must be your own. See Pace University's academic integrity policy.
